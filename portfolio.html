MoveTogether
taxi-sharing service
Ridesharing is a difficult two-sided, supply-driven market problem. It's hard to incentivize drivers to offer a ride as they prize convenience and their quiet, personal time. However, ridesharing was more successful amongst close-knit communities. Therefore, my team pivoted to a one-sided market: taxi-sharing amongst the business community. We prototyped at the GoogleI/0 conference in 2011, matching rides to and from SFO. It was a big success.
#start-up #user-research #mock-ups #user-experience #cross-team-implementation #management

National Grocery Chain
monitor consulting
This Canadian national grocery chain was experiencing declining pharmacy sales. My team designed and conducted a consumer research program consisting of an online survey, customer interviews, shop-alongs and focus groups to better understand the problem. The number one driver of loyalty seemed to be superior pharmacists and customer care. We put together a plan to better train pharmacists and to automate pharmacy processes so pharmacists can spend more time with patients.
#user-research #user-experience #management
