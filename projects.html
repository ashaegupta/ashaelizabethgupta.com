Sigma-Dek
DIY Outdoor Deck
People desire the emotional satisfaction of building a deck themselves instead of contracting it out. But they want to be able to build the deck in a weekend and without many obscure tools. Therefore, the team prototyped (and eventually built) a simple snap-in-place deck kit. Pieces snap together, but are still stable, safe and strong, and the entire deck can be built in 45 minutes.
